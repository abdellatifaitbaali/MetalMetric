Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Metal Metric ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Metal Metric collects minimal data to provide our services:

  • User Preferences: Currency selection and display settings stored locally on your device
  • API Keys: If you provide a custom API key, it is stored locally on your device
  • Usage Data: We do not collect analytics or usage data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Display precious metal prices in your preferred currency
  • Remember your app settings between sessions
  • Fetch price data from third-party APIs

4. Third-Party Services

Metal Metric uses the FreeGoldPrice API to fetch precious metal prices. When you use our app, requests are made to their servers. Please review their privacy policy for information about their data practices.

5. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that store your personal information. You can delete all app data at any time through the Settings screen.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information stored on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
